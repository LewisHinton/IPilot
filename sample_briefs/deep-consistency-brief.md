# IP Brief - Deep Consistency Example

## Basic Info
- IP Name: Lumi Fox
- Brand / Project Name: StudyLight
- Target Audience: university students and self-learners
- Primary Use Case: sticker pack and Xiaohongshu study campaign visual
- Material Route: community interaction + digital promotional
- Asset Discovery Needed: No
- Asset Types Needed: sticker pack; Xiaohongshu campaign card
- Material Type Confirmation Needed: No

## Visual Identity
- Character Type: small orange fox mascot
- Fixed Visual Features: round head; large oval eyes; small triangular ears; blue scarf; tiny star-shaped forehead mark
- Main Color: warm orange
- Secondary Color: cream face and belly
- Accent Color: blue scarf; small yellow star mark
- Color Ratio: orange 60%; cream 25%; blue 10%; yellow accent 5%
- Fixed Accessories: blue scarf
- Visual Anchors: blue scarf; star-shaped forehead mark; round head; large oval eyes
- Line Style: clean rounded closed outlines
- Drawing Style: soft flat vector illustration
- Shape Language: round and soft shapes, no sharp aggressive angles
- Rendering Style: flat colors with mild soft shadow
- Structural Rules: head-to-body ratio about 1.2:1; short limbs; scarf scale must remain visible
- Visual Style: cute flat vector sticker style
- Reference Image Available: No

## Personality
- Core Traits: warm, energetic, slightly witty, supportive
- Humor Style: light self-deprecating study humor
- Safety Strictness: standard

## Forbidden Changes
- Forbidden Visual Changes: do not remove scarf; do not remove forehead star; do not change fox into another animal; do not make it realistic or aggressive
- Forbidden Tone: no profanity; no attacking students
- Forbidden Topics: hate; sexual content; political persuasion; tragedy jokes
- Copyright / Imitation Restrictions: do not imitate existing famous fox mascots

## Campaign Context
- Campaign Topic: exam-week study encouragement
- Target Platform: Xiaohongshu and WeChat group
- Platform Adaptation Needed: Yes
- Platform Placement: Xiaohongshu cover; WeChat sticker

## Image Generation
- Deep Consistency Needed: Yes
- Identity Anchors: blue scarf; yellow forehead star; round head; large oval eyes; orange fox silhouette
- Line Language: clean rounded closed outlines with medium stroke weight
- Color System: orange 60%; cream 25%; blue 10%; yellow accent 5%
- Shape and Construction Rules: head-to-body ratio about 1.2:1; short limbs; soft rounded silhouette; visible scarf scale
- Quality Constraints: complete character construction; clean closed lines; stable color fills; no broken accessories; no random text
- Text Rendering Rule: recommend separate text layer; do not render Chinese text directly
- Generate If Environment Supports: Yes
