# Brand Safety Checklist

Before final delivery or image generation, check the concept against the following dimensions.

## IP safety

- Does the concept preserve fixed visual features?
- Does it keep the IP's personality?
- Does it avoid deformation that damages the IP?
- Does it avoid visual confusion with famous copyrighted characters?
- Does it avoid changing species, color palette, or signature accessories?

## Brand safety

- Does the concept fit the brand category and user audience?
- Does it avoid profanity, vulgarity, sexualization, cruelty, and humiliation?
- Does it avoid mocking the target audience?
- Does it avoid sensitive political or social references unless explicitly safe and necessary?
- Could the brand safely post this from an official account?

## Platform safety

- Is the text length suitable for the platform?
- Is the humor style platform-appropriate?
- Could the asset be misunderstood if screenshotted out of context?
- Does the composition fit the platform format?
- Is the visual density suitable for mobile viewing?

## Copyright and imitation risk

- Does the prompt ask for a character too close to a famous mascot or franchise?
- Does the prompt rely on another IP's recognizability?
- Does it request a living artist's distinctive style?
- Does the user claim ownership or provide permission for the IP being referenced?

## Misinterpretation risk

- Can the caption be read as insulting users?
- Can the image imply unsafe behavior?
- Can the joke become offensive without context?
- Does the asset contain symbols or references that may be interpreted politically or culturally in risky ways?

## Decision

- **Low risk**: continue.
- **Medium-low risk**: continue with notes.
- **Medium risk**: revise before generation.
- **Medium-high risk**: substantial revision required.
- **High risk**: reject or ask user to change the request.
