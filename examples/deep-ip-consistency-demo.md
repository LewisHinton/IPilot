# Demo - Deep IP Consistency Review

## Scenario

The user already owns an IP named Lumi Fox and wants a sticker pack plus a Xiaohongshu study campaign visual. The main pain point is that generated images often change the fox's color, line style, or scarf shape.

## Deep IP Consistency Profile

### Identity Anchors

- small orange fox mascot;
- blue scarf;
- star-shaped forehead mark;
- round head;
- large oval eyes.

### Line Language

- clean rounded closed outlines;
- medium stroke weight;
- low internal detail density;
- no sketchy or noisy lines.

### Drawing Style

- soft flat vector illustration;
- cute sticker-ready proportions;
- mild soft shadow only;
- no realistic fur texture.

### Color System

- orange 60%;
- cream 25%;
- blue scarf 10%;
- yellow star accent 5%.

### Flexible Motion Space

Allowed:

- sleepy expression;
- excited expression;
- holding coffee;
- holding flashcards;
- sitting at desk;
- running to class;
- library, dorm, classroom, coffee shop backgrounds.

Not allowed:

- removing scarf;
- replacing star mark;
- changing into a wolf/cat/dog;
- realistic aggressive fox style;
- heavy dark horror lighting.

## Asset Concept

### Sticker: "鎴戣繕鑳藉"

- Asset Type: WeChat sticker
- Scene: Lumi Fox hugs a small notebook while sitting beside a coffee cup.
- IP Action: sitting, slightly trembling but determined.
- Facial Expression: tired but still energetic.
- Caption: 鎴戣繕鑳藉
- Text Handling: caption as separate text layer; do not render Chinese text directly in generated image.

### Visual Prompt

Create a WeChat sticker-style image featuring Lumi Fox, a small orange fox mascot with a round head, large oval eyes, a blue scarf, and a small yellow star-shaped mark on the forehead. Preserve the clean rounded closed outlines, soft flat vector illustration style, and color ratio: orange about 60%, cream about 25%, blue scarf about 10%, yellow accent about 5%. Lumi Fox is sitting beside a coffee cup, hugging a small notebook, with a tired but determined expression. Keep the character complete, structurally coherent, and sticker-ready. Use clean color fills, mild soft shadow, low background detail, and no random text. Do not remove the blue scarf or forehead star. Do not change the fox into another animal. Do not use realistic fur or aggressive style.
