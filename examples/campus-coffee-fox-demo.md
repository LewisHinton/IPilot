# Demo: Xiaoju for Campus Coffee Brand

## User request

We already have a campus coffee mascot called Xiaoju. Please create exam-week stickers and social assets that feel warm, funny, and brand-safe.

## IP Brief

- IP Name: 小橘 / Xiaoju
- Brand / Project: Campus coffee brand
- Target Audience: university students, exam-prep students, late-night learners
- Character Type: orange fox mascot
- Fixed Visual Features:
  - orange fox
  - round face
  - large eyes
  - blue scarf
  - soft chibi proportions
- Personality:
  - energetic
  - companion-like
  - mildly sarcastic
  - never insulting
- Platform: WeChat stickers, Xiaohongshu cover images, campaign posters
- Campaign Topic: exam-week coffee promotion
- Forbidden Changes:
  - do not remove the blue scarf
  - do not depict alcohol, smoking, or unsafe stimulant behavior
  - do not insult tired students
  - do not use profanity
